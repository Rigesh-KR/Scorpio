In this role, you will be responsible for sourcing and procurement strategies by reviewing and managing supplier performance to ensure competitiveness in term of quality, cost and delivery.

Responsibilities:

  • Manage supplier identification, supplier sourcing/selection, negotiation, agreement and supplier performance
  • Coordinate with supplier quality development and purchasing teams to prepare monthly supplier performance reports and to share the information to suppliers
  • Perform part cost structure analysis to achieve best price based on required specification
  • Supplier profile and material info record (e.g. price, lead time, payment term, MOQ/SPQ) management in ERP system
  • Develop, implement and drive the procurement strategies in order to meet cost savings targets
  • Assist engineering team to find and develop suitable supplier candidates
  • Clarify to supplier on price discrepancy topic between invoice and agreed price

Requirements:

  • Diploma/Bachelor Degree in Mechanical, Electrical, or Industrial
  • Experience in engineering and procurement
  • Able to read and understand part drawings and requirements
  • Effective verbal and written communication skills
  • Have a good knowledges and skills in production process and BoM cost analysis (Mechanical parts – e.g. stamping, plastic injection, rubber, and casting; Electrical parts – e.g. PCBA, module, HMI, and battery system)
  • Proficiency with personal computers, specifically Microsoft Excel
  • Knowledge and direct experience in negotiating contracts and quotations with suppliers
  • Able to travel local and overseas for supplier visits and audits
  • Candidates with relevant years of experience will be considered for senior positions

  • Understanding of typical materials and processes used for plastic prototyping and mass production
  • Experience in cross-functional development including joining to metal components as well as electromechanical assembly
  • Experience in organizational standards such as ISO 9001, and automotive standard procedures in APQP (including PPAP, PSW)
  • Experience with product development cycle for plastic components including prototyping, first article, part approval process, testing
  • Able to learn and work independently as well as team player in a lean environment
  • Good written and oral communication skill
